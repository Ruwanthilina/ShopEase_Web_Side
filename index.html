About Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is a stunning island nation in South Asia, located in the Indian Ocean. Known for its breathtaking landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture, it is home to ancient temples, lush tea plantations, and diverse wildlife.From the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress to the serene beaches of Mirissa, Sri Lanka offers a unique mix of adventure, history, and natural beauty. It is also one of the world's leading producers of tea.

Must-Visit Places

Sigiriya Rock Fortress, an ancient marvel and UNESCO World Heritage Site.

The beautiful hills of Ella, famous for breathtaking train rides.

Mirissa Beach, known for its golden sand and whale watching tours.

Yala National Park, home to elephants, leopards, and exotic wildlife.

Galle harbour was a major harbour in the country, which was in use even during the pre-Christian times.

Narangala mountain in Sri Lanka has an elevation of 1,520 meters (4,986 feet).
