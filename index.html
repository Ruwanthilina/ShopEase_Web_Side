About Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is a stunning island nation in South Asia, located in the Indian Ocean. Known for its breathtaking landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture, it is home to ancient temples, lush tea plantations, and diverse wildlife.

From the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress to the serene beaches of Mirissa, Sri Lanka offers a unique mix of adventure, history, and natural beauty. It is also one of the world's leading producers of tea.
